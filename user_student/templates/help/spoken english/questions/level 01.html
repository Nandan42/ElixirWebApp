What are Questions? This itself is a Question!

A question is a sentence that seeks an answer for information, collection, tests and research. One of the most important tasks in speaking any language is asking questions. When you ask questions, you receive information.

There are different types of questions in English: questions that can be answered with a simple yes or no, questions that can be answered using prior knowledge and questions that require more detailed analysis.

One type of Questions are those with 5W and 1H. These are WHY, WHO, WHAT, WHEN, WHERE and HOW

Examples

  • What is your name?
  • How are you?
  • Who is this?
  • Where are you?
  • When will you play?
  • Why did you go?
  • What are the colours in the rainbow?

Apart from this, we can also ask questions as below

  • Do you sing?
  • Will you play a game of badminton with me?
  • Are you hungry?
  • Can you run?
