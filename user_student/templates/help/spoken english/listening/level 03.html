Listening – Level 3

This level mainly covers pronunciation of a combination of words. It also helps in understanding common vocabulary and expressions in short, clear dialogues. Situations include simple explanations, introductions, messages and announcements.

Example

“once in a blue moon”, “devil’s advocate”, “The best of both worlds”, “ I’m fine, thanks. How about you?” “Pretty good”, “Same as always”, “Not so great.”, Could be better”, “Can’t complain”
