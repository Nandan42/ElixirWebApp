Listening – Level 4

This level mainly covers pronunciation of sentences. A sentence is a group of words that are put together to mean something.

Example

“My name is Abhishek”,
“I stay in Bangalore”
“I study in Bangalore University”
“Where are you from?”
“Where do you stay?”
“What’s the cost of this shirt?”
“How far is the marketplace from here?”
