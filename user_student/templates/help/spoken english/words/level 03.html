Words-Level 3 Lesson

Common Type of Words

English has four major word classes:
nouns,verbs,adjectives and adverbs

     1. Noun:

A noun is a person, place, thing, quality, or act.

Examples: Raj, Bangalore, shoes, turtle, anger, sadness, town

     2. Verb:

Verbs are action that tell what the nouns do.

Examples:

  • Raj went to Bangalore
  • Raj Laughed
  • The Turtle walked slowly

     3. Adjective:

An adjective describes a noun.

Examples:

  • bald, heavy, useful
  • The Turtle is heavy

      4. Adverb:

An adverb describes a verb, adjective, or adverb. It often ends in 'ly'.

Examples:

  • simply, patiently
  • She drives carefully
  • They lived happily
  • The cricket team played badly last Saturday
